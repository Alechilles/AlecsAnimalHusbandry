# Getting Started Guide

Parent: [Start Here](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/start-here) | [Home](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/animal-husbandry-wiki)

tl;dr: 
Livestock and Neutral wildlife are tamed identically to base Hytale: feed their favorite food. Aggressive predators (Beasts) require more steps to weaken and capture, akin to Pokemon or Monster Hunter. 

After taming, link neutral animals with [Animal Control Flute](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/animal-control-flute), and predators with [Combat Beast Flute](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/combat-beast-flute), to manage their health and happiness. Right-click with flute to open the command interface. Left-click with flute to link animals and issue group commands. Interact (default keybind F) will manage most other interactions. Craft flutes at farming bench.

Animals need access to food, water, and protected space to roam and socialize. Passive breeding occurs with high happiness, and is toggled per-animal in the command flute interface. 

Animals will only eat their preferred food (used for taming), or generic feed crafted at farming bench. Generic feed inhibits breeding due to unhappiness, but they won't starve.

## Beast Taming
1. Craft Tranquilizer Potion at Lv2 Alchemy workbench 
   (Note: [Glowing Purple Mushrooms](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/glowing-purple-mushroom-spores) can be cultivated with seeds crafted at Lv6 Farming Bench)
2. Craft Tranquilizer Shortbow and Tranquilizer Arrows at Lv2 Weapon Bench.
3. Weaken beast to < 20% HP, then shoot tranquilizer arrow until it sleeps.
4. Feed preferred food to sleeping beast to complete the taming process.
5. Link beast with [Combat Beast Flute](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/combat-beast-flute) to manage health and happiness, and command up to 3 Active beasts at a time in combat.
   (Note: Flute requires Wild Wisteria wood, grown from Wild Wisteria sapling, Lv6 Farming Bench).

# Health and Happiness 

Hunger and thirst decay over time

Feed animals by placing food in a feed trough or chest (legacy). 
Fill a trough with water by right-clicking with water bucket. Note: animals can drink from water source block, but may drown. This is base Hytale behavior). 
Animals must have room to move

## Step 3: Build A Stable Enclosure (Both Paths)
1. Give enough room for movement.
2. Add safe access to water.
3. Place nearby storage with suitable food items.
4. Keep shelter and pathing clear.

Why this matters:
- Hunger and thirst decay over time.
- Animals will automatically seek out food and water from nearby storage and water sources.

## Step 4: Stabilize Needs And Happiness
Default decay rates:
- Hunger: **1 per minute**
- Thirst: **1.3 per minute**

Breeding readiness depends on happiness plus eligibility gates.

> [Screenshot Placeholder: Companion stats panel showing hunger/thirst/happiness]

## Step 5: Start Breeding Intentionally
1. Keep compatible animals close enough.
2. Avoid overcrowding.
3. Maintain high care quality.
4. Track growth cycles so you can plan your next generation.
5. If you want to trigger breeding manually, **crouch and interact while holding the animal's preferred food**.

Use the detailed timings here:
- [Breeding and Growth Guide](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/breeding-and-growth-guide)

## Step 6: Scale With Commands
Use command items to control multiple companions efficiently:
- Positioning (Follow, Hold, Move To Ping)
- Recovery (Recall, Return Home)
- Beast combat behavior (flute command set)
- Quick mode toggle: press `F` on a tamed companion to cycle simple modes (`Follow`, `Hold`, `Idle`).
