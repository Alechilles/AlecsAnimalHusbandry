# Glowing Purple Mushroom Spores

Parent: [Items Index](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/items-index) | [Home](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/animal-husbandry-wiki)

**Glowing Purple Mushroom Spores** let you turn a scarce tranquilizer ingredient into a renewable, farmable crop.

## What They Are For
- Grow more **Glowing Purple Mushrooms** for the [Tranquilizer Potion](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/tranquilizer-potion) recipe.
- Sustain your long-term Beast taming supply without relying only on naturally found mushrooms.

## Recipe
- 1x Glowing Purple Mushroom
- 8x Life Essence
- 1x Void Essence

## Bench Requirement
- Farming Bench (Seeds, Tier 6)

## Craft Time
- Instant

## Output
- 1

## How To Use
1. Craft the spores at the Farming Bench after you have harvested at least one Glowing Purple Mushroom.
2. Plant them in **mud**.
3. Keep them **out of sunlight** for the fastest growth.
4. Harvest the mature mushroom and use it to brew [Tranquilizer Potion](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/tranquilizer-potion).

## Growth Notes
- Fastest growth happens in low sunlight.
- Direct sunlight slows growth _very_ heavily, but does not completely stop it.
- Current growth profile is **5 stages** and about **2 days** in low sunlight.

## Why They Matter
- Each [Tranquilizer Potion](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/tranquilizer-potion) needs **2x Glowing Purple Mushroom**.
- That potion then feeds into both [Tranquilizer Arrows](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/tranquilizer-arrows) and the [Tranquilizer Shortbow](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/tranquilizer-shortbow).
