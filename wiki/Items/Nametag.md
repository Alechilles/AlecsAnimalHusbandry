# Nametag

Parent: [Items Index](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/items-index) | [Home](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/)

![Nametag](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/70ad5a86-bd2f-4655-805e-85dc9fbef1a5)

The **Nametag** is provided by **Alec's Nametags!** and is included in the Animal Husbandry dependency set.
Use it to name your tamed livestock and Beasts so your stable and command panel are easier to manage.

## Mod Requirement
- Requires **Alec's Nametags!** to be installed alongside Animal Husbandry and Alec's Tamework.

## Crafting
- Crafting station: **Farmer's Workbench**
- Recipe:
  - 2x **Light Leather**
  - 1x **Gold Bar**
- Craft time: **1 second**
- Max stack: **1**

## How To Use
1. Hold a **Nametag**.
2. Left click a valid tamed companion.
3. Enter a custom name in the naming prompt.
4. Confirm to apply the new name.

## Default Rules
- Target must be **tamed**.
- Naming normally requires ownership.
- Unowned tamed NPCs are also allowed by default config.
- Existing names can be replaced.
- Allowed characters: letters, numbers, spaces.
- Name length: 1 to 24 characters.
- Item is consumed on use.
