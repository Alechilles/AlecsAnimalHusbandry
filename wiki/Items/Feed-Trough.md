# Feed Trough

Parent: [Items Index](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/items-index) | [Home](/mod/alecs-animal-husbandry/)

![Feed Trough](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/29f3f0b6-8f5c-4b77-a3a4-9c83feebb2a0)

The **Feed Trough** is a care block that helps automate food and hydration support in pens.

## What It Does
- Can store food or water to support nearby animals.
- Works well for enclosed pens where natural water placement is awkward.
- The feed/water level will visibly decrease as supply decreases.

## Feed Trough Recipe
- 10x Any Wood
- Output: **1**
- Crafting station: **Farming Bench (Farming category)**
- Craft time: **1 second**

## Bucket Recipe (For Trough Refills)
- 3x Any Wood
- 1x Iron Bar
- Output: **1**
- Crafting station: **Farming Bench (Farming category)**
- Craft time: **1 second**

## How To Use
![Fill Feed Trough Water](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/909a99c1-282a-47f2-b5f1-38610c278e36) ![Fill Feed Trough Food](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/999d908b-423f-47ea-a8cc-3056dc73a762)

1. Place a Feed Trough in your enclosure.
2. Fill the trough.
   - Interact to store food like a normal chest.
   - Right-click with a bucket of water to fill with water.
3. Keep it stocked to keep companions fed and watered.

## Usage Tips
- Keep troughs inside fenced pens and close to your animals.
- Don't put troughs against single-height fences or animals may climb out.
