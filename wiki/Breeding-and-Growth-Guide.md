# Breeding and Growth Guide

This page is your practical timing reference for line planning.

## How Breeding Is Gated
Your companion must pass all of these checks:
- Tamed
- Adult
- Not in combat
- Not sleeping
- Meets happiness threshold (default: **70**)
- Not blocked by cooldown/delay windows
- Not blocked by nearby same-type limits

## Planning Tips
1. Fast-growth species are great for rapid trait experiments.
2. Long-cycle species should be treated as premium bloodlines.
3. High Beast cooldown species benefit from tight pairing management.
4. Use role limits (Max Nearby Same Type) to avoid overcrowding penalties.
